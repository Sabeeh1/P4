Gain Weight

Gaining weight is definitely one of the hardest goals to reach. But we will help you make it a reality. \

  • 1) Check your body weight and your height
  • 2) Once you have both, take a look at our graph
  • 3) Look for your body weight and height and take a look at the amount of calories you should be consuming
  • 4) Once you've got your diet down, take a look at our exercises (exercises are for both those losing/gaining weight)
  • 5) Get started and don't quit!

    • Lose Weight

    Losing weight is more of a mental game than physical. Compared to gaining wieght, losing weight takes a on your mental if you're overweight and trying to cut down

  • 1) Check your body weight and height
  • 2) Once you have both, take a look at our graph
  • 3) look for your body weight and check the amount of calories you should be consuming and burning
  • 4) Take a look at the exercises and focus on cardio
  • 5) Get started and don't quit!

    • Gain Muscle

    Gaining muscle depends more on protein intake and the amount of exercise you're doing.

    • 1) Make sure your protein intake is at least over 60g a day
    • 2) Go to the gym often and have a solid split (I recommend push, pull, legs)
    • 3) This is the most important, stay consistent
